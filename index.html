About Us

General Automotive Workshop

is an automotive service provider that offers different types of services ranging from maintenance and repairing up to upgrading your most valued car.

GAWS is a specialist for all electronic transmission, EUROPEAN, AMERICAN, and ASIAN automotive industry.

Mr. Cesar E. Salcedo

started his career in Automotive industry in Torino, Italy for the past 27 years. He has a vast experience of working with different types of automotive services.

Along with his passion and knowledge in this industry, he established his own business in January 10, 2019 with the vision of providing an excellent auto services in a affordable way to his fellow Filipinos.
